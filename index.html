Our Magical Mentors

Our teachers whispered unspeakable knowledge and pulled us through the cosmic horror of senior high... MWEHEHE!

Sir. Flor

Practical Research 2

Guides us through the alchemy of quantitative research methods, teaching how to design, conduct, and present research studies. His wisdom illuminates our academic path like a magical lantern in the dark!

Sir. Niel

CPAR & 21st Century Literature

(CPAR) Unlocks the mysteries of Philippine arts from different regions, fostering appreciation for local artistic expressions and cultural heritage. (21st CL) Examines literary works with a modern haunting twist that chills to the bone!

Sir. Makaraig

Understanding Culture, Society and Politics

Investigates human cultural variation, social differences, and political identities to develop cultural awareness and sensitivity. His lessons reveal the hidden patterns of society like ancient runes!

Sir. Torres

Physical Education and Health

Focuses on fitness, sports, and health education to promote lifelong wellness and physical activity. Teaches us to outrun even the fastest ghosts and ghouls!

Ma'am. Araneta

Physical Science

Covers basic concepts of chemistry and physics, including matter, motion, energy, and the universe. Reveals the magical properties that make our world both terrifying and wonderful!

Ma'am. Ravis

EAPP & MIL

(EAPP) Develops communication skills in academic and professional contexts, emphasizing critical reading and writing. (MIL) Teaches how to navigate the digital spirit world responsibly in the haunted age of technology!

Ma'am. Viana

Filipino sa Piling Larang (FPL)

Enhances Filipino language skills in specific fields or tracks, focusing on technical and professional applications. Unlocks the ancient secrets of our language with modern magical applications!

Sir. Vincent

Business Finance

Introduces financial management principles, investment decisions, and financial planning for businesses. Teaches the dark arts of turning mere coins into haunted fortunes!

Ma'am. Rochelle

Marketing & Business Ethics

Principles of Marketing: Covers fundamental marketing concepts, strategies, and consumer behavior in business contexts. Business Ethics: Examines ethical decision-making and corporate social responsibility in haunted business environments!

Ma'am. Ramos

FABM 2

Provides foundational knowledge in accounting, business, and management principles for ABM students. Unlocks the mysterious language of numbers that governs the business underworld!

Sir. Erik

Work Immersion

Provides hands-on experience in real work environments to develop skills and prepare students for future careers. Guides us through the haunted corporate labyrinths with practical wisdom!
